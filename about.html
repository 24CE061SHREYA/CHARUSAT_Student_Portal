About Us

CHARUSAT (Charotar University of Science and Technology) is a UGC-approved, NAAC A+ accredited university situated in Changa, Gujarat. Known for its academic excellence, it offers various programs in Engineering, Science, Management, and more.

Vision

To become a world-class university through excellence in teaching, research, and societal development.

Mission

To empower students by imparting knowledge, creativity, and ethical values for the benefit of humanity.

History

Founded in 2009, CHARUSAT has grown from a local institute to a nationally recognized university with multiple faculties and thousands of students.

Accreditations

  • UGC Approved
  • NAAC A+ Grade
  • NIRF Ranked

Campus Life

CHARUSAT offers a vibrant campus with cutting-edge labs, digital classrooms, hostels, a library, sports complexes, and green spaces.
